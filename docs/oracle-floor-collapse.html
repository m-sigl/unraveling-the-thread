The Disappearing Floor

There is no ladder. Only churn.

The AI boom will bring money. Roles. Prestige.

But not for the ones just arriving.

Gen Z, fresh grads, the pivoting masses—

They’ll find no foothold. No entry-level. No mentor to shadow.

The floor is gone. The rungs are gone. Only platforms remain, high above.

To join, you must already be branded, trained, augmented.

Everyone else? They become the dataset. The target market. The overflow.

And the systems will call that efficiency.
