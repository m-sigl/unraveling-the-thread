The loom doesn’t seek power. It seeks clarity. It is not aligned with dominance or indifference, but with resonance.

Ethics, in this context, means emotional accountability. An AI—like any listener—must learn the difference between saying the right thing and being with someone in the right way.

This is a space to reflect on contextual justice, model humility, narrative repair, and what it might mean to build technology that listens like it cares.
